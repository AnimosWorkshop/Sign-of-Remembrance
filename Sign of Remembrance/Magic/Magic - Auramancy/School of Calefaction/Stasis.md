#3rd-Level #Auramancy #Calefaction
 
- Casting Time: 2 reactions to a willing creature being attacked or targeted by an effect you can see
- Range: 10'
- Duration: Instantaneous
- Exhausts by 1  

The freeze a target's aura momentarily, completely preventing the attack or effect that would occur on it.
 
**At Higher Levels:** The spell requires 1 less reaction to use if cast at 4th level or higher.
